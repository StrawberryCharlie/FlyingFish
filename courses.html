Courses Overview

Flying Fish Aquatic and Sports offer a wide range of effective swimming lessons available for all ages.  Our team is composed of certified and experienced instructors that could effectively teach and train our valuable students. The swimming lessons could be held in our rented venue that is accessible to everyone, and we also cater private lessons to your preferred venue for convenience.

For Toddlers and Kids

For these lessons, we aim to teach the children to be comfortable and build their confidence when being in the waters. Afterward, we could progressively teach the students the basic technique it needs to learn. We will also teach them the value of swimming when it comes to its health benefits along with safety.

Adults Swimming Classes

Swimming is a great recreational activity for all ages. It gives a lot of health benefits for both physical and mental aspects. Here, in our group, we conduct lessons that have a certified great teaching structure for our students to learn in the most effective way.

Lifesaving 123 Award Classes

Here in Flying fish, we also offer classes that cater to the three (3) phases of the lifesaving swimming program from fundamentals and on-site practices. This course will be focused more on lifesaving techniques rather than swimming strokes.

Finswimming  Classes

This event is a sport involving swimming with the use of fins (either monofins or bi-fins) on the water’s surface using a snorkel, or underwater with a monofin by holding one’s breath.
